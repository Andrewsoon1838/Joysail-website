Document Workflows Supported

Invoice Processing

Process invoices across multiple formats—capturing, validating, and structuring key data into finance systems.

Reduces manual entry, improves accuracy, and accelerates reconciliation cycles.

Order-to-Cash Processing

Process purchase orders, confirmations, and related documents—structuring them into ERP and fulfilment systems.

Eliminates manual order entry, reduces delays, and improves order accuracy across workflows.

Proof of Delivery & Exception Handling

Process delivery notes (DO), goods receipt notes (GRN), acknowledgements, and discrepancy reports across formats—validating and structuring them into logistics and warehouse systems.

Eliminates manual verification and follow-ups, improving turnaround time and control across fulfilment workflows.

Supplier Onboarding

Capture and structure supplier information from forms, emails, and supporting documents—integrating them into procurement and vendor management systems.

Reduces onboarding time, improves data consistency, and ensures better compliance across records.

Document Consolidation

Consolidate documents from multiple sources—emails, PDFs, and attachments—into a unified, structured workflow.

Improves visibility, reduces fragmentation, and ensures consistent data flow across systems.

Returns & Reverse Logistics

Process return requests, credit notes, and supporting documents—structuring them into inventory and finance systems.

Reduces processing delays, improves tracking, and strengthens control across reverse logistics workflows.
